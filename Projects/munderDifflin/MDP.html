About

Dunder Mifflin Paper Co. is a Stationery and lifestyle brand based in Queens, New York. Funded and owned by the Perez brothers. The company officially launched in 2015 via dundermifflinpaperco.com. What began as a small business in Jonas, Erick and Jose's apartment has quickly grown in to an international brand.

DM Paper co's roots are in stationery, but we continue growing and developing new products to bring you endlessly fresh, truly unique art, stationery, and home decor you won't find anywhere else. With curated designs through in house competitions.
